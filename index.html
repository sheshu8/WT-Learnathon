LandScaping Gardening

Landscaping refers to any activity that modifies the visible features of an area of land, including the following: Living elements, such as flora or fauna; or what is commonly called gardening, the art and craft of growing plants with a goal of creating a beauty within the landscape.

Vertical Garden

A vertical garden is a vertically suspended panel on which plants are grown using hydroponics. These unique structures can either be freestanding or attached to a wall and go by many different names: living green walls, plant walls and moss walls just to name a few.

Interior Gardening

The concept of indoor gardening is pretty straightforward: It's the act of growing produce inside your home. Maybe you choose to garden indoors because of a lack of outdoor space, cold temperatures or maybe you just want to pick fresh food from the comfort of your kitchen.
